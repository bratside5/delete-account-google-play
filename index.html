Data Safety and Account Deletion

At futur73, we take your data safety seriously. If you wish to delete your account, please follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Log into your account.
  2. Go to the 'Account Settings' section.
  3. Click on 'Delete My Account'.
  4. Confirm your decision.

Please note that deleting your account will result in the removal of all personal data associated with your account. This includes your profile information. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

If you have any further questions or concerns regarding data safety, please contact our support team. futur73@proton.me
